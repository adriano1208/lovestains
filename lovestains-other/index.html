About Us

Join us as we explore The Lovestains epic journey.

  • May 2015

    A Rock Band is Formed

    Song writing, practicing, playing at school events were all part of the early days for The Lovestains' band members, however the band itself was not formalized in its current form until May 2015 when Joao Machado (Guitar), James Beswick (Bass) and Chris Gough (Guitar) sought out a drummer to join their band and stumbled across a University acquaintance namely Tim Rokebrand. Tim then brought along AP Mlomzale to sing in one of The Lovestains jam sessions. The result was electric!




  • The Past Year

    Jamming Hard

    Over the last year to the present day The Lovestains have been playing gigs in and around Johannesburg, South Africa. The heart of The Lovestains has been established as a core of five guys that love playing music together. Our policy is to ensure that nobody leaves a gig without their minds blown and thus far we have managed to achieve that goal. Furthermore we like clean floors on which people can dance erotically. That is all.




  • Present Day

    Chilling Hard

    We also enjoy kicking back and drinking a beer or ten. This is us at one of our jam sessions taking in the sights and doing what we love.
